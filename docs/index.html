Mission Statement

I hope to combine my major and minor to create websites that schools and others can use to help educate people about Black history that basic elementry and high school books do not teach. I feel like this is important because Black students are mostly taught about slavery, Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., the Civil Rights Movement and thats pretty much it. It does not go into dept about excatly why or how slave trade came into existes. It does not teach the truths of slavery and how things the Black community says and do now are connected to it. It does not teach about the historic harm picnics had on Black people and excatly why they are called picnics. It does not teach about all the different activist groups formed during The Civil Rights Movement. There is alot more that those books and the edcuation system does not teach Black students, but these kind of things are very important because they are our history.
