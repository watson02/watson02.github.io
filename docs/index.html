Mission Statement

I hope to combine my major and minor to create websites that schools and others can use to help educate others about Black history that basic elementary and high school books fail to acknowledge. I believe that this is important because Black students are mostly taught about slavery, Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., and the Civil Rights Movement, and that's pretty much it. It does not go into depth about exactly why or how the slave trade came into existence. It does not teach the truths of slavery and how certain things within the Black community say and do now are connected to it. It does not teach about the historic harm things like picnics had on Black people and exactly why they are called picnics. It does not teach about all the different activist groups formed during The Civil Rights Movement. There is a lot more that the history books and the education system does not teach others.
